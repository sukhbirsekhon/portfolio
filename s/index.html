About Me

Hey, I am Sukhbir Sekhon, an BSIT student at University of Cincinnati and currently enrolled in MBA program as well. Well, there are a lot of things that I can talk about myself, but I believe that there are three 3 things you should know about me. I was born in India and spent 16 years there. For 16 years, I lived in a competitive and learning environment where I learned that giving up is not an option. I educated myself in programming and gained ethics and morals from teachers, family, and friends. Those ethics made me better human being. I learned to help others, respect everyone, keep learning, do what I love to do, and be loyal.
