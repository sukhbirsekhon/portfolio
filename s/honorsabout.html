About Me

I am a student at University of Cincinnati in Information Technology major with Software Application Development as a main track. I didn't know how to speak english before, but I learned it to interact with people and to communicate my thoughts with other people. I learn quickly and that is my strength. I learned how to speak new language. One of my main goal was to work under professional and excellent mentor, which I found in Information Technology Solutions Center. Dr. Hazem Said is my mentor. He offered me a job at ITSC and I get a lot of meaningful experience by working under his supervision.  I am a normal kid who wants to support my parents in all ways I can, wants to get knowledge in IT, work with experienced and passionate people, achieve my goals, and work at big IT company. I found out that I am good at adopting new technologies at work and work around them. I am always curious to work with new technologies because it fascinates me how well they work. I like to work with back-end development along node.js and angular.js programming languages.

I not only want be good with computers but also with people because what's the use of all of it if it can't even solve human problems. I like to work around people who always looks forward to help people by solving complex problems. I want to explore all the positive ways in which I can help people around community.
